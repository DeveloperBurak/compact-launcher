Welcome To Compact Launcher

You can easily launch your programs in this little rocket!

This program aims to provide all your shortcuts in that rocket, when you click or hover on this, you can see all apps that you imported

How App Works

Compact Launcher folder is added in your Documents. When you move your shortcuts to shortcuts folder, this shortuct appears in list when you expand the screen through rocket. Also if you want to preview image for this shortcut, you can move your image to images folder same name with shortcut

Preferences

There is some preferences for you. You can determine that how the app would act. to set your preferences before application starts. You can change them any time.

You are ready to go!
